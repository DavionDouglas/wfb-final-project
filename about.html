How we got Started!

Starting from the ground up with a year of woodshop experince earned in high school, owning a skateboard company was my number 1 dreram. With a bunch of skate buddies and supporters, we were able to create a company that caters to skateboards all across the globe. Our main focus is to make sure every skaters needs are met when visiting our site or skateshop. The skateboarding community is like no other. If you share the same deep love & passion for skateboarding like we do, come on & take a stroll with us.

Skate Camp

Every other summer, we give skateboarders of all ages to come and experience the time of thier lives. Offered at camp is a week long stay in our fine establishment, skate lessons, woodshop class to create and take home your own deck, and many other activities. Come back next year to RSVP your spot at our camp. Happy trails!

Be apart of the team!

We accept sponsor me tapes no matter how good or bad you think you are. Remember, we tend to be our ownselves worst critic so don't sweat it. Not all skaters get sponsored right away but that doesn't mean you won't in the near future. Skateboarding is all about fun so dont beat yourself up. We will always be here waiting for your tape.
